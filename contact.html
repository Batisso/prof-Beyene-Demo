Check Out Some Of The Frequently Asked Questions

Who was Professor Beyene Petros?

Professor Beyene Petros (1948–2023) was an esteemed Ethiopian scholar, scientist, public servant, and advocate for education and social development. He dedicated his life to advancing scientific research, improving higher education, and championing equitable access to opportunities in Ethiopia. Known for his integrity, leadership, and commitment to his community, Professor Beyene’s work has inspired countless individuals across Ethiopia and beyond.

What is the mission of the Beyene Petros Foundation?

The mission of the Beyene Petros Foundation is to honor the life and legacy of Professor Beyene Petros by advancing education, research, and community development in Ethiopia. Through scholarships, capacity-building initiatives, and community programs, the foundation seeks to empower individuals and institutions to foster sustainable development and social equity.

How does the foundation support education?

The foundation prioritizes educational initiatives by:
- Offering scholarships to underprivileged students in Ethiopia.
- Supporting research projects in the fields of science, technology, and public health.
- Partnering with universities and institutions to improve access to quality education and infrastructure.

What are the foundation’s key focus areas?

The Beyene Petros Foundation focuses on three primary areas:
- Education and Scholarships: Supporting students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
- Scientific Research and Innovation: Promoting research in public health, agriculture, and environmental sustainability.
- Community Development: Implementing grassroots projects to improve livelihoods and social well-being.

How can I get involved in supporting the foundation?

There are several ways to support the Beyene Petros Foundation:

- Donate: Contributions help fund scholarships, research grants, and community programs.
- Volunteer: Share your expertise or time to support foundation initiatives.
- Partner with Us: Collaborate on educational, scientific, or community-based projects.
Visit the Contact page on our website to get in touch.

Where does the foundation operate?

The Beyene Petros Foundation is headquartered in Ethiopia and focuses primarily on projects within Ethiopia. However, its impact stretches globally through partnerships, research collaborations, and diaspora support.

Who oversees the foundation's activities?

The foundation is governed by The Beyene Petros family and a board of trustees composed of professionals, academics, and community leaders who share Professor Beyene’s vision for a better Ethiopia. The board ensures transparency, accountability, and the effective implementation of all initiatives.

About us
The Professor Beyene Petros Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a brighter, more equitable future for Ethiopians and Africans by advancing education, fostering scientific research, and empowering communities. Inspired by the life and legacy of Professor Beyene Petros, a visionary scholar, educator, and advocate for sustainable development, the Foundation carries forward his lifelong mission of creating opportunities and addressing the pressing challenges of our time.
