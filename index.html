About Foundation

The Professor Beyene Petros Foundation is dedicated to honoring the life, vision, and enduring contributions of one of Ethiopia’s most distinguished scholars, educators, and advocates for social justice. Professor Beyene Petros left an indelible mark on Ethiopia’s academic, political, and humanitarian landscapes. Through this foundation, we aim to continue his work by fostering education, research, and community development.

About

Professor Beyene Petros was a beacon of hope and progress for Ethiopia and beyond. His dedication to knowledge, democracy, and building a just society continues to inspire people across generations.

Who was Professor Beyene Petros?
Professor Beyene Petros (1950–2024) was an esteemed Ethiopian scholar, scientist, public servant, and advocate for education and social development. He dedicated his life to advancing scientific research, improving higher education, and championing equitable access to opportunities in Ethiopia. Known for his integrity, leadership, and commitment to his community, Professor Beyene’s work has inspired countless individuals across Ethiopia and beyond.
What is the mission of the Beyene Petros Foundation?
The mission of the Beyene Petros Foundation is to honor the life and legacy of Professor Beyene Petros by advancing education, research, and community development in Ethiopia. Through scholarships, capacity-building initiatives, and community programs, the foundation seeks to empower individuals and institutions to foster sustainable development and social equity.
What are the foundation’s key focus areas?
The Beyene Petros Foundation focuses on three primary areas:

Education and Scholarships: Supporting students from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Scientific Research and Innovation: Promoting research in public health, agriculture, and environmental sustainability.

Community Development: Implementing grassroots projects to improve livelihoods and social well-being.

Testimonials

Heartfelt stories and reflections from those
who have worked with or been inspired by him.

“Professor Beyene has been my colleague since I joined the university. But I first met him when I was his student. I remember him having many post graduate and doctorate advisee students in the field of biology, and I saw him making successful, the lot of them. He has such a great passion and skill in coordinating organizing and leading research works. When it is a research work, whether it is one he is undertaking or one conducted by his students, he does it to perfection.”
Professor Yealem Tsehay Mekonen
Biology professor & researcher at Addis Ababa University
“I know Beyene from childhood. He was studying at Atse Gelawdewos when I was working at Adama. He has consistent personality from that time to date. He joined the politics to be the voice of the oppressed. A barn of Blessings cannot shrink however much is taken from it ; Beyene is like that. The fact that his efforts for the well being of the people are recognized now at his age of 70 is a great pleasure. He never said ‘see me!’ or ‘learn from me!’. But who ever decides to look at him is sure to take valuable lessons.”
Tesfatsion Delelew (PHD)
Close Friend
“Because his father moved to places on duty, Beyene grew up with his grandparents and with us. We, his uncles, sent him to school at his early age. He completed sixth grade in only three years. As a politician Beyene was liked by the people but pushed by the government. My wish was to see the fruits of his struggle in politics like I did see in his education and Professorship. The whole Hadiya and the whole Ethiopia is proud of the fact that Beyene is a truthful politician who was never swayed by personal gains and who stood firm for the people.”
Azimach Desalegn
Uncle, Hadiya Elderly Man
“He never brings himself first. He only thinks ways of making contribution. As a wife I would have preferred it if he took some vacation, but he doesn’t accept that. He says we have not made any change. He is such a perfectionist that he wants to excel at everything he does. He has many surprising personalities. He loves his work and he manages allocating time carefully for his work and for his social commitments. In our forty years of married life he follows up each movement of his children and gives support. He never keeps away from responsibility.”
Tihun wolde
Wife
“ He kept reminding each one of us the need to focus on our studies. I am a teacher and a PhD candidate on peace research. I think this is influenced by my dad. There were times when I would travel with him as his driver during times of election movements. On the spot he showed me the reality of the country for the change of which we should strive. He re focuses and gets back to his work even when encountering provocation and pressures.”
Ermisha Beyene
Son

About us
The Professor Beyene Petros Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a brighter, more equitable future for Ethiopians and Africans by advancing education, fostering scientific research, and empowering communities. Inspired by the life and legacy of Professor Beyene Petros, a visionary scholar, educator, and advocate for sustainable development, the Foundation carries forward his lifelong mission of creating opportunities and addressing the pressing challenges of our time.
