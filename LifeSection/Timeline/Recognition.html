Professor Beyene Petros's illustrious career has earned him numerous awards and honors.


These accolades reflect his enduring commitment to excellence and his ability to inspire others through his work.


Some of them include:

- An international diploma of laureate, in the catagory of Lifetime tropical diseases (Like Malaria) researcher, University professor and Politician higher honor laureate,


- Certificate of merit & Gold medal, in the recognition of his outstanding performance in public health service/senior public health research,


- The Year 2013 Good human award, in the catagory of University professorship and teaching.

Behind the accolades and professional achievements lies a man of great humility and compassion. Those who know Professor Petros personally describe him as a dedicated family man, a loyal friend, and a mentor who always puts others before himself.
His ability to balance a demanding career with a deep sense of humanity is one of the many traits that make him truly remarkable.
His legacy is one of hope, resilience, and an unshakable belief in the power of knowledge to change lives.
