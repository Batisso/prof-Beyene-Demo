Member of

Parliament

Professor Beyene served as a member of Ethiopia’s House of People’s Representatives, where he represented his constituency with diligence and advocated for policies that prioritized education, healthcare, and rural development.
As an opposition leader in parliament, he often challenged the government on issues such as corruption, authoritarianism, and human rights abuses. Despite facing significant political pressure, he remained steadfast in his commitment to democratic values.

Member Of
Parliament

Throughout his political career as a member of the parliament, Professor Beyene faced numerous challenges. Like many opposition leaders in Ethiopia, he was subjected to government surveillance, intimidation, and even imprisonment.


However, these hardships only strengthened his resolve to fight for a more inclusive and democratic Ethiopia. His resilience earned him widespread respect, both within Ethiopia and internationally.
