About us
The Professor Beyene Petros Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to building a brighter, more equitable future for Ethiopians and Africans by advancing education, fostering scientific research, and empowering communities. Inspired by the life and legacy of Professor Beyene Petros, a visionary scholar, educator, and advocate for sustainable development, the Foundation carries forward his lifelong mission of creating opportunities and addressing the pressing challenges of our time.
